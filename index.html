Rihanna Just Marked Her Return to the Stage in Summer’s Most Divisive Trend

By Olivia Allen July 14, 2026

Rihanna made a surprise return to the stage at Yankee Stadium, joining Jay-Z during his 30th-anniversary tour—and she did it in a pair of Saint Laurent capri pants, one of summer’s most talked-about silhouettes.

Cropped trousers have been resurfacing all season, championed by the likes of Bella Hadid, Emily Ratajkowski, and Beyoncé. As British Vogue recently put it, in a summer of exposed everything, the calf has quietly become the season’s new focal point.

Rather than lean into the polished “clean-girl” version of the trend, Rihanna reached for three-quarter lengths and an off-the-shoulder top that recalled her Music of the Sun era—a reminder that capris were pop-royalty uniform long before the current revival.
